KILL THE VC - PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the "Kill the VC" game ("Software").

Camera Data: The Software accesses your device's camera to enable hand gesture controls. This video data is processed in real-time locally on your device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to any external servers.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve the Software, including game session duration, feature usage, and error reports.

We use the information we collect to:

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

We may share anonymous, aggregated information with our partners for the purpose of improving the Software.

4. DATA RETENTION

Camera data is processed in real-time and is not stored. Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for a reasonable period to allow for analysis and improvement of the Software.
